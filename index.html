Too Hype To Handle

A Bipolar Two Survival Game

       

TOO HYPE TO HANDLE, A BIPOLAR TWO SURVIVAL GAME

is a simulation based on what it's like to live with Bipolar Two. Players can perform various actions - sleep, eat, indulge, exercise, work, reflect - in response to different scenarios.

The effects of the player's choice is reflected in the mood status bar. Actions will preserve the player's mental health status as baseline - or push the player into either a depressive or hypomanic mood. Depending on what state the player is in, their action choices may be limited.

The goal of this application is to gamify the process of healthy living and mood disorder management.
